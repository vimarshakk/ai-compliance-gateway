## Script

**[0:00-0:15] Hook**

> Every day, millions of AI requests go through your app. Some contain patient data. Some contain credit cards. Some contain prompt injection attacks. And you have zero visibility into what's happening.

**[0:15-0:45] Problem**

> AI compliance isn't optional anymore. India's DPDP Act, US HIPAA, EU GDPR — regulations are catching up. But most teams are building AI features without any compliance layer. They're flying blind.

**[0:45-1:15] Solution**

> AI Compliance Gateway is Cloudflare for your AI stack. It sits between your app and your AI providers. Every request passes through policy checks, risk scoring, PII detection, and audit logging. You get compliance scores, cost tracking, and full visibility.

**[1:15-1:45] How It Works**

> 1. Your app sends a request to ACG
> 2. ACG checks it against your compliance packs — DPDP, HIPAA, GDPR
> 3. PII is detected and masked
> 4. Risk score is calculated
> 5. Request is forwarded to your AI provider
> 6. Response is checked for policy violations
> 7. Everything is logged for audit

**[1:45-2:00] CTA**

> Start in 5 minutes. `npm install -g @acg/cli && acg scan`. Open source. Free tier available.
